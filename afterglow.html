Afterglow

Student Run Digital Music Publication @ UT Austin

Overview

Afterglow is a student-run digital and print music publication founded in fall of 2018 at the University of Texas at Austin. As one of the founding members, I was tasked with building the brand identity and website for the organization, as well as other assets like merch and design guidelines.

My Role: Creative Director; UX/UI (squarespace.com), graphic design, layout design, brand identity
