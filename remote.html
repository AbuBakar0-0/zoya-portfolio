Overview

A smart TV remote app that lets users control their TV, cast media, and browse content — all in one place.

The goal was to replace traditional remotes with a simple, unified mobile experience.

Click here to jump to the designs! ↓

Problem

Users face everyday frustrations with smart TVs:

  • Lost or broken remotes
  • Multiple apps for control and casting
  • Complicated connection process
  • Inconsistent experience across devices

Solution

Designed a single app that:

  • Replaces the physical remote
  • Enables quick and seamless casting
  • Centralizes media and controls
  • Works across multiple devices

Key Features

  • Remote Control (touch + button modes)
  • Screen Mirroring / Casting
  • Media Browser (photos & videos)
  • App Shortcuts
  • Customization options
  • Multi-language support
  • Monetization integration

Design Approach

  • Kept UI minimal and familiar (TV-like controls)
  • Reduced steps for connection & casting
  • Focused on quick access and responsiveness
  • Used clear visual feedback for user actions

Outcome & Learnings

  • Users prefer all-in-one solutions
  • Fast connection = better retention
  • Casting should be a primary feature
  • Simplicity improves usability
  • Real-time performance is critical

Final Result

A streamlined and user-friendly TV remote experience that combines control, casting, and media into one cohesive app.
