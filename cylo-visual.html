Project Overview

Cylo – Period Tracker is designed to help users easily monitor their menstrual cycles, track symptoms, and gain meaningful health insights. The app focuses on simplicity, clarity, and a calm visual experience to make daily tracking effortless and informative.

Design Approach

  • Clean and calming UI design
  • Focus on readability and clarity
  • Soft and soothing color palette
  • Simple and intuitive navigation
  • Personalized and user-centric experience

Final Outcome

A user-friendly and visually balanced Play Store presentation that highlights the app’s core value—helping users track their cycles with ease, clarity, and confidence through a calm and intuitive experience.
